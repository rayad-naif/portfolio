The Vision

We don't just take photos.
We craft legacies.

Aura Studios was born from a desire to freeze time in its most elegant form. Whether it's the intimate quiet of a portrait or the grandeur of a wedding day, we bring a cinematic eye to every frame.

Our approach is minimalist, focusing on light, shadow, and raw emotion. We believe beauty lies in authenticity.

FAQ

How far in advance should I book? +

For weddings, we recommend booking 9-12 months in advance. For portrait sessions, 3-4 weeks is usually sufficient.

Do you travel for shoots? +

Absolutely. We are based in NYC but travel worldwide. Travel fees are calculated based on location.

What is your turnaround time? +

For portraits, 2 weeks. For weddings and large events, 6-8 weeks for the full gallery.
